Website Plan

Site Name

Name: Weaving Our Legacy

Why: Like weaving a tapestry, Family history is often found or learned about one person at a time.

Site Pupose

This site will be a place to share research, stories, and answer questions about my family history with other relatives who share my lineage. I can also answer family history research questions or help solve difficult family history problems.

Scenarios

I want to learn more about my maternal great-grandmother. What can you tell me about her?

How can I get started on family history research?

I can't find my grandfather's birth information. Can you help me?
