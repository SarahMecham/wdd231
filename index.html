About Me

Hi, my name is Sarah. I live with my husband and kids in South Jordan, Utah. I have two daughters (19 & 12) and three sons (17, 15, & 9). I love spending time with my family; they bring me joy! We love playing games, hiking, biking, and snowboarding/skiing together. I am an avid runner and mountain biker. I love racing in triathlons and just racing in general. I just ran a half-marathon last Saturday, and after some much-needed rest, I will begin training for my next marathon. I love moving and seeing how far I can go. For personal hobbies, I enjoy cooking, woodworking/building (I have built about 50% of the furniture in my house), and anything crafty (knitting, sewing, painting). I love family history, and it has been the focus of my education. I also enjoy web and computer programming, though they stretch me in ways I never knew possible!
